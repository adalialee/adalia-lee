My Projects

Take a look at some of the tech-related projects I’ve made, and the skills I’ve developed along the way!

How to Increase Your Iron Intake (Website)

As my Experience and Interaction final project, I developed an educational website about how to increase iron intake, which identifies and addresses several target audience groups that are more likely to be iron-deficient. My website features multiple pages (I broke down the steps by page) and interactive animations to increase user engagement.

Cat. (App)

In collaboration with my Oasis NEU team, I built an app that promotes productivity with the incentive of caring for a virtual cat, where simulated health levels shift based on user-provided data about task completion. I designed several front-end and back-end features such as adding to and keeping track of a to-do list. This was my teammates and I's first time using Swift!

EcoKeen (App)

In high school, I developed a carbon footprint tracker application with a team from our Women in Tech Club to combat climate change in a more approachable manner. Our application generates data visualizations of a user's carbon footprint based on their energy usage, and features a personalized recommendation system for tips/alternatives to reduce their carbon footprints. I was involved with the wireframes, branding, business plan, coding, and presentation to judges for our Technovation Challenge entry.
